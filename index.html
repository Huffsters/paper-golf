How to play

Get from the tee to the flag in as few strokes as you can. Each turn, tap Roll 🎲 — that’s how far the ball flies. Then tap one of the 8 targets to aim, and tap it again to swing.

You can always putt 1 square — tap one of the small green dots, no roll needed (putting without rolling saves your draw; once you’ve rolled, that die is spent either way). Perfect for tapping in when the flag is close.

Two extra safety nets: your first shot gets one free reroll (but you’re then committed to the new roll), and every round has 2 🔄 mulligans you can spend to reroll any shot.

Your lie adjusts the roll:

  • Fairway (mowed green): roll +1. Stay on the short grass!
  • Rough (pale, tufty): no bonus.
  • Sand: roll −1.
  • Trees block your ball — it drops short with a thunk. Sometimes wasting a shot into them is the smart play…
  • Water costs a penalty stroke, and you replay from where you swung. The ball flies over water — only where it lands matters.
  • Land exactly on the hole to sink it.

Everyone draws from the same dice and gets the same rerolls, so it’s skill (mostly). Par is fair — every hole is checked to be beatable with its rolls, and a perfect round scores a Birdie. Reach par + 5 and you pick up — a ✗ that breaks your streak.

⏱ The daily round is timed, from your first swing to the final putt. Fewest strokes tops the leaderboard 🏆 — ties go to the faster golfer.

A new hole drops at midnight. Meanwhile: free play deals endless practice holes, and the editor ✏️ lets you draw your own hole and share it as a link.
