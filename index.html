How to play

Get from the tee to the flag in as few strokes as you can. Pick a club — each flies a set distance — then tap a target to aim, and tap it again to swing.

  • Trees block your ball — it drops short with a thunk.
  • Water costs a penalty stroke, and you replay the shot from where you swung. The ball flies over water — only where it lands matters.
  • Sand plugs your ball — your next shot must be a Wedge or Putter.
  • Land exactly on the hole to sink it.

Par is fair: every hole is checked to be beatable, and a perfect round scores a Birdie. If you reach par + 5 strokes, you pick up — a ✗ that breaks your streak.

⏱ The daily round is timed, from your first swing to the final putt. Fewest strokes tops the leaderboard 🏆 — ties go to the faster golfer.

Everyone plays the same hole each day, with a new one at midnight. Warming up? Free play (below the clubs) deals endless practice holes that don’t count.
