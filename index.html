How to play

Get the ball from the tee to the flag in as few strokes as you can. Pick a club (how far the ball flies), tap a target, then tap it again to swing.

  • Trees block your ball — it drops short with a thunk.
  • Water costs a penalty stroke and you replay from where you swung. (The ball flies over water if it lands beyond it.)
  • Sand plugs your ball — your next shot must be a Wedge or Putter.
  • Land exactly on the hole to sink it.

Par is set fair — every hole is checked by a solver, and a perfect round scores a birdie. Reach par +5 and you pick up. ✗

Everyone in the world plays the same hole each day. Come back tomorrow to grow your streak. 🔥
